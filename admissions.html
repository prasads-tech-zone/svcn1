Eligibility for MCA


A pass in bachelor's degree in any field with 45% marks in aggrgate. Must have qualified in Integrated Common Entrance Test (ICET) conducted by AP State Council of Higher Education, Govt of AP. The candidates should satisfy local/non-local status requirement as laid down in the AP Educational institutions (Regulation of Admissions) Order 1974 as amended from time to time.

The Convener admits 70% of the intake to the MBA programme based on the ICET rank. The Management admits the remaining 30% under management and NRI quota.



Documents to be submitted at the time of admission:


  • School Leaving Certificate / Transfer Certificate from the school or College last attended.
  • SSLC, PUC Or Equivalent marks card.
  • Study Certificate.
  • Caste certificate. (If applicable)
  • Income Certificate(If applicable)
  • Recent Passport size photographs 6 Nos.
  • Address Proof & Adhar Card
  • Antiragging affidavit.
